The second brother, finally realizing his mistakes, became a respected person, deserving of his position.
The village was not convinced, but after a moving speech and the taking down of the wall to symbolize their new freedom, they were willing to give him a second chance.
Our hero did not stay for long, he had regained his spirit for adventuring, and the village was in good hands.
Their father would come home to a flourishing village once more.
And so, he left, with a smile on his face.
No one has seen ever again the evil pink rabbit again.
