He was greeted by some friendly traders just outside who happened to recognize him, but what they said didn't make him happy.
His father was gone, and a new 'brother' of his was ruling in his stead, leading the village to chaos.
It couldn't go on, so as his father's son he resolved to solve this problem.
He would travel through the village, now crowded with roaming soldiers, and reach the mansion, then stop his misguided brother.
