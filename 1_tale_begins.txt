Our tale takes place in a faraway faraway land, where fireflies dance in the skies and clouds form artistic shapes like they had a will of their own.
Our town, a large commercially successful community, was flourishing. New trade routes were established every day, children played between the houses, families were harvesting well...
Like all things, it couldn't last. Their benevolent and wise mayor vanished, without a trace, and his son took his place.
The son wanted to expand the village even more, create an empire, but he had none of his late father's charisma. It infuriated him, he broke off promises, drew up new restricting laws, it all took its toll in the village.
Trading became scarce, children's laughter was rare, a solemn air had fallen on the village. Something had to happen, 
someone had to do something, but what.......
